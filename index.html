F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

ByteBrains Technology's mission is to create new possibilities for people and organizations everywhere.

  • What can I get help with?

    You can get on-demand or scheduled tutoring in hundreds of subjects and test-prep areas, including AP® subjects, algebra, writing, programming languages, world languages, web design, and NCLEX®. If there’s a subject in which you need help—or just want an extra boost—we are here to support you. All of our most popular subjects are available in Spanish, too.

  • Are tutors qualified in their subjects?

    Our tutors are highly qualified. They go through a rigorous vetting process, including education verification and multiple background checks. They also have to pass a subject matter exam and complete an interview. Plus, we have a quality specialist team that reviews sessions to ensure our tutors are always providing the highest level of support.

  • What is available in the online classroom?

    You can typically choose to connect with a tutor via two-way text-chat or voice—whichever is more comfortable for you. Our virtual classroom has all the tools you need to make the most of your session: two-way whiteboard, code editor, text editor, and graph paper—plus a range of drawing tools, graphing calculator, formulas, file sharing, and emoji.

  • What should I expect from my session?

    Our expert tutors provide encouraging, empowering support. We follow a Socratic method of teaching that helps guide students through the learning process. Your tutor will work with you to help you understand how to solve problems on your own. We’ve been doing this for decades, and learners regularly report that their tutors are patient, supportive, helpful, and kind. After your session, you’ll be equipped with knowledge to help you with future assignments. And if you ever need help, you can always connect with us.

  • Is my session safe?

    Yes. We comply with all applicable safety and security regulations. Our tutors are rigorously vetted, and our quality specialist team ensures the integrity of our sessions. Your tutor does not receive any personal information about you, and your sessions are anonymous and secure.
